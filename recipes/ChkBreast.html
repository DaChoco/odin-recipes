Chicken Breast

Some simple fried chicken

Ingredients

  • Rosemary
  • 1tbsp Olive oil
  • Thyme
  • Coriander
  • Salt
  • BBQ spice

Steps

  1. Cut chicken in half
  2. add thyme coriander and bbq spice on both sides
  3. Cut shallow into the top of the chicken breast
  4. Get pan and oil to it then heat it on the stove
  5. Add chicken to pan
  6. Let it brown then dig in
