Bolognaise Recipe

Bolognaise is an italian dish consisting of mince, pasta tomato paste and other ingredients which shall be listed here.

Ingredients

  • Beef Mince
  • tomato
  • 1tbsp Olive Oil
  • Spaghetti
  • Parmesean
  • Garlic
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Get a Sauce pan at medium heat and pour the tbsp of olive oil into it
  2. Reduce the heat and add the onions, carrots, celery sticks, garlic cloves , then fry for 10 mins. Stir the veg often until it softens.
  3. Increase the heat to medium-high, add 500g beef mince and cook stirring for 3-4 mins until the meat is browned
  4. Add 2 tins plum tomatoes, the leaves from ¾ small pack basil, 1 tsp dried oregano, 2 bay leaves, 2 tbsp tomato purée, 1 beef stock cube, 1 deseeded and finely chopped red chilli (if using), halved cherry tomatoes. Stir with a wooden spoon, breaking up the plum tomatoes
  5. Bring to the boil, reduce to a gentle simmer and cover with a lid. Cook for 1 hr 15 mins stirring occasionally, until you have a rich, thick sauce.
  6. When the bolognese is nearly finished, cook 400g spaghetti following the pack instructions.
  7. Drain the spaghetti and either stir into the bolognese sauce, or serve the sauce on top. Serve with more grated parmesan,
