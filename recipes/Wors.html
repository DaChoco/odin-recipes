Some simple fried wors

Ingredients

  • Raw Boerewors
  • 1tbsp Olive oil
  • Rosemary
  • BBQ spice

Steps

  1. Defrost the Wors if frozen
  2. Add bbq spice and rosemary to wors
  3. Cut the roll into pieces that can fit on the pan
  4. Optional: Prick the sausage with a fork to make it less juicy if its your preference
  5. Let it brown then eat
